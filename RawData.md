# Smart Contract Data Inventory

This document tracks all raw data sources downloaded and stored in the `output/` directory.

## 1. Audit Reports & Public Reviews
Extensive collection of security audit reports from various firms and platforms.

- **Nethermind Audits**: `output/repos/audit_repos/PublicAuditReports`
- **Hexens Audits & Reviews**: 
    - `output/repos/audit_repos/public-audits`
    - `output/repos/audit_repos/Smart-Contract-Review-Public-Reports`
- **Cyfrin Audits**: `output/repos/audit_repos/cyfrin-audit-reports`
- **Sherlock Audits**: `output/repos/audit_repos/sherlock-reports`
- **MixBytes Audits**: `output/repos/audit_repos/audits_public`
- **Callisto Network Audits**: `output/repos/audit_repos/Auditing`
- **Consolidated Audit Collections**:
    - `output/repos/audit_repos/PublicReports` (CosmWasm, Solana, Move, NEAR, ZK, etc.)
    - `output/repos/audit_repos/audit-reports` (Individual firm reports)
    - `output/repos/audit_repos/Smart-Contract-Audit-Reports`
    - `output/repos/audit_repos/Smart-Contract-Audits` (Chronological collection)
    - `output/repos/audit_repos/audits` (Solo and team audits)

## 3. Kaggle Datasets
Tabular and code-based datasets from Kaggle.

- **Smart Contract Vulnerability Dataset (tranduongminhdai)**: `output/datasets/kaggle/tranduongminhdai_smart-contract-vulnerability-datset`
    - Includes `SC_Vuln_8label.csv` and `SC_4label.csv`
- **BCCC VolSCs 2023**: `output/datasets/kaggle/bcccdatasets_bccc-vulscs-2023`
    - Includes secure and vulnerable source codes with corresponding CSVs.
